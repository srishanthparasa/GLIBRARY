About Glibrary

Glibrary is my personal game library — a simple place to track what I play, what I’ve finished, and what I’m excited to try next.

I love a mix of genres: RPGs, Soulslikes, action adventures, and indie gems. This site helps me keep everything organized with quick filters, a clean dark theme, and space for little notes and ratings.

It’s built with plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript using localStorage for persistence. No frameworks — just lightweight and fast.
